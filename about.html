Steffany Gross

Steffany is an only slightly Overwatch League obsessed nerd who loves video games, computers, and generally all things nerdy. You can check out her other work here.

The World Needs Heroes is a basic memory match game that brings in some of the surprises and excitement of the Overwatch game. Ultimates, surprise consequences, and awesome music. And Bastion.

This game was made with HTML, CSS, and Javascript. Also some vaguely suspect Photoshop skills, serious Google-fu, and a whole lot of trial and error.
